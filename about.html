ABOUT

Hello, I’m Maayan

Before I write down the obvious that design is part of
my life here are some facts about me:

- I like to laugh and have fun.

- I love to travel around the world and I've been on
three continents.

- I love learning new things and always being involved.

- I love fashion design and I’m sewing on my free time.

- I'm mad about dogs!

So now that you to know a bit about me I can continue
to tell that I graduated four years on Avni Institute of
Art and Design in Tel Aviv, studying visual
communication, my love of design touches every
aspect of my life and it influences the things I do or
face against.

My design style is rich and I cannot define it.

I believe that graphic design has changed the world
and will continue to do so in conveying messages in a
beautiful and correct way and today in the digital age
we are developing and can have a greater impact on
the daily experience and influence it by making it
more useful, beautiful and more accessible.
