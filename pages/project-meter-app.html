Meter App

A convenient and easy to use application where
you can find parking lots according to the area you are in.
You can identify your prefered parking lots by looking at the entrance
image. There is a price list that give you more clarity about all
the prices so you will not be surprised when you enter the parking lot.
The app interfaces to the parking lot machine, so you can choose to pay in
cash or use your saved credit card information.
